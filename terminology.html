How to Play
The best

way to play is to utilize three things. Double-tap, safepoint, and one-ways.

Terminology
Double-tap

Double-tap is an exploit that was found in 2019, it allows two or more shots (depending on the weapon) to be fired within a single frame/tick.


Desync

Desync is an exploit that came after Fake-Angles was patched in December of 2018. This allows you to desync your playermodel by choking packets sent to the game. With Fake-Angles you could create a fake up to 360 degrees from your real player-model. However, with desync you can only create a fake up to 60 degrees from your playermodel.


Safepoint

Safepoint is a feature that creates a matrix for -60 degrees from 180, +60 from 180, and 180 to find all the possible points in the body that will not be affected by whether or not you have the enemy resolved. It is best to attempt to force safepoints only and use doubletap to kill the enemy player within one tick without missing.
