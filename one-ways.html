One-ways
On this page

we will go over what a one-way is and the best way to utilize them.

Different One-ways.
What is a one-way.

A one-way is an area where you can shoot from one side and do damage but they can't shoot back.


How you should play in them.

Different types of one-ways require different ways of holding them. Some one-ways you will need to lower the minimum damage that you are attempting to shoot for. In other areas you need to crouch. I personally like to crouch in most one-ways if they're able to be shot through while crouching, just because crouching can cause animation bugs in some cheats that can mess with their resolver.
