More Terminology
On this page

we will go over more terms that you need to know before playing.

Terminology
Resolver

A resolver is something that utilizes animation info on a player to attempt to bruteforce to find the real player-model's position. Resolvers are very useful and are very much blamed for a lot of issues.


Multi-point

Multi-point is a feature that allows the cheat to scan for points all around the body part rather than aiming for the center of the bone. You have the option to scan further from the center based on a point-scale. Putting this on 100% is a very bad idea, because there could be other things that can cause you to not know where the player-model actually is, and if you try to shoot too far from the center than you have a much higher chance of missing.


Lag-compensation

Lag-compensation is a feature that allows you to shoot at past records of a player and still hit them if it is a valid lag record. The further away a lag record is, the less of a chance you have of actually hitting it before it becomes invalid.
